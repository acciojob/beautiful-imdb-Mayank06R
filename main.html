Marco is a 2024 action thriller film about a family of gold traders in Kerala, India. The film follows the story of Marco, the younger brother of the family head, George, who sets out to find the truth behind a shocking incident that affects his family. Plot: The Adattu family, one of Kerala's most well-known gold-trading families, is shaken by an unexpected incident. George, the family head, tries to find the truth and those responsible. Marco, George's younger brother, also tries to find the truth but takes a different path.
